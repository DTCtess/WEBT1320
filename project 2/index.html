art

My art style is highly influenced by folk art and graffiti across the world, as well as psychedelic and surreal modern art. I create unique paintings in acrylic and gouache as well as calligraphic inspired graffiti in a variety of mediums. My paintings are usually a juxtaposition of new and old, dark and cheerful, or classic and avant garde. I love to combine unusual items together.
